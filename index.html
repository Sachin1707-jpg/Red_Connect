Why RedConnect

Smart Features for
Faster Life Saving

From GPS-based donor matching to real-time emergency alerts — RedConnect has everything needed to connect blood to those who need it.

📍
GPS Smart Matching
Haversine formula calculates real distances. Nearest available donors with matching blood group are surfaced instantly.
🚨
Emergency Alerts
Critical cases trigger instant push notifications to all nearby eligible donors within seconds.
🏥
Hospital Dashboard
Hospitals can create requests, manage inventory, track responses, and view emergency cases all in one place.
🎖️
Rewards & Badges
Donors earn points and badges for each successful donation — gamifying life-saving to drive more participation.
🗺️
Interactive Map
Visual map showing hospitals, blood requests, and nearby donors in real-time across the entire region.
🏢
NGO Coordination
NGOs can manage donation camps, coordinate volunteers, and view blood shortage alerts across cities.

How It Works
